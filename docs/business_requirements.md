# Business Requirements

## Objective
Analyze customer shopping behavior and identify patterns that can improve sales, customer retention, and marketing effectiveness.

## Business Questions

1. Which customer segments generate the highest revenue?
2. Which product categories are most popular?
3. What shopping trends occur over time?
4. How does spending vary by age and gender?
5. Which customers are at risk of churn?
6. What recommendations can improve customer engagement?

## Success Criteria

- Clean dataset prepared
- SQL analysis completed
- Dashboard created in Power BI
- Actionable recommendations generated
